# Conversion Strategy (CRO)

## 🧠 Objective
Maximize the number and quality of leads across all pages by:
- reducing friction
- increasing trust
- offering fast contact options
- guiding users clearly toward action

---

## 🎯 Core Conversion Principles
1. Always visible contact options
2. Reduce steps to conversion (max 2–3)
3. Mobile-first interaction
4. Clear and strong CTAs
5. Trust before asking for data

---

## 📲 Primary Conversion Channels

### 1. WhatsApp (Highest Priority)
- Floating button (all pages)
- Inline buttons (hero, property pages, CTAs)
👉 Fastest and most effective conversion method

---

### 2. Contact Forms
- Short forms only
- Minimal required fields
👉 Used for:
- Sell page
- Contact page
- PVC page
- Property pages

---

### 3. Phone Calls
- Always visible
- Especially important for high-intent users

---

## 🧩 Conversion by Page Type

### 🏠 Home
- Hero with strong CTA
- Service cards (Buy / Sell / Rent / PVC)
- CTA blocks between sections
- WhatsApp visible

---

### 💰 Sell Property (MOST IMPORTANT PAGE)
👉 This is the main revenue driver
Must include:
- Strong headline (free valuation)
- Short form (name + phone)
- Trust elements
- WhatsApp + phone
- Urgency triggers

---

### 🏠 Property Pages
- WhatsApp button near top
- Contact form below details
- "Request info" CTA
- Similar properties (keep user engaged)

---

### 🏡 Buy / Rent Pages
- Filters (reduce friction)
- Property cards with clear CTA
- WhatsApp option visible

---

### 🪟 PVC Windows
- Benefits (ROI, energy saving)
- Visual proof
- CTA blocks
- Form + WhatsApp

---

## 🔥 Psychological Triggers

### Urgency
- "Limited properties available"
- "High demand area"

---

### Social Proof
- Testimonials
- Real cases
- Local presence

---

### Clarity
- Simple language
- Clear value
- No confusion

---

### Trust
- Local expertise
- Professional look
- Transparent process

---

## 📏 Form Rules
- Max 3–4 fields
- Mobile-friendly
- Fast submission
- Clear success message

---

## 📍 CTA Strategy
CTAs must:
- be repeated across the page
- use action-driven text
- stand out visually

### Examples:
- "Solicitar valoración gratuita"
- "Hablar por WhatsApp"
- "Ver propiedades"
- "Solicitar información"

---

## 🔁 Conversion Loop
User journey:
Traffic → Interest → Contact → Follow-up → Close → Upsell
👉 Website must support this flow at every stage

---

## ⚠️ CRO Rules
- No page without CTA
- No dead ends
- No long forms
- No hidden contact options
- Always prioritize speed and clarity

---

## 🎯 Final Goal
Create a website where:
- users understand what to do instantly
- contact options are always visible
- friction is minimal
- conversion is natural and fast
