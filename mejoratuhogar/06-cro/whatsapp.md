# WhatsApp Strategy

## 🧠 Objective
Use WhatsApp as the primary and fastest lead conversion channel across the entire website.

---

## 📲 Core Role
WhatsApp is:
- The main conversion tool
- The fastest way to contact
- The preferred channel for mobile users

👉 It must be present and accessible at all times

---

## 📍 Placement

### Global
- Floating button (visible on all pages)
- Always accessible on mobile and desktop

---

### Strategic Locations
- Hero sections
- CTA blocks
- Property pages (top + bottom)
- Sell page (high priority)
- PVC page
- Contact page

---

## 💬 Message Strategy

### Default Message
"Hola, estoy interesado en sus servicios."

---

### Contextual Messages (Recommended)
👉 The message should change depending on the page:

#### Buy page
"Hola, estoy interesado en comprar una vivienda."

#### Property page
"Hola, estoy interesado en esta propiedad."

#### Sell page
"Hola, quiero vender mi vivienda."

#### PVC Windows page
"Hola, quiero información sobre ventanas PVC."

👉 This increases conversion significantly

---

## ⚡ Response Strategy
- Respond as fast as possible
- Prefer immediate replies
- Keep communication simple and direct

👉 Speed = conversion

---

## 🧠 Psychological Advantage
WhatsApp reduces:
- friction
- hesitation
- form fatigue

Users feel:
- closer
- more confident
- more comfortable asking questions

---

## 🔁 Integration with CRO
WhatsApp must:
- complement forms (not replace them)
- be present in all key flows
- act as the fastest shortcut to contact

---

## ⚙️ Technical Implementation
- Use direct WhatsApp API link:
  https://wa.me/[number]?text=[message]
- Pre-fill message dynamically based on page context
- Ensure:
  - mobile optimization
  - fast loading
  - no blocking scripts

---

## 📊 Tracking
Track:
- WhatsApp clicks
- Conversion rate from WhatsApp
- Page origin of click

👉 Use Google Analytics or similar

---

## ⚠️ Rules
- Always visible
- Never intrusive
- Always relevant to context
- Message must be clear and simple

---

## 🎯 Final Goal
Make WhatsApp the easiest and fastest way for users to:
- ask questions
- request information
- start a conversation

👉 and convert into leads immediately
